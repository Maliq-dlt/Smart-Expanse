## 2024-05-02 - Icon Accessibility Enhancements
**Learning:** Combining `aria-label`, `title`, `focus-visible` styling, and `aria-hidden="true"` on inner material-symbol elements creates a robust and accessible UX for icon-only interactive elements across navigation components.
**Action:** Consistently apply this pattern to all new icon-only buttons to ensure they provide helpful tooltips to sighted users, are identifiable by screen readers (without double reading), and support clear keyboard navigation.
